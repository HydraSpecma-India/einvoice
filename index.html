e-Invoice Forge · User Guide

Welcome to the e-Invoice Forge client-side application. Follow this simple guide to process your Dynamics 365 Indian invoices:

Step 00: Local Folder Setup [1-Time Setup]

Click Select PDF Folder and Select History Folder in the left pane to set your local working directories. This gives the browser permission to read invoices and write signed backup PDFs. Paths are cached in your browser.

Step 01: Drag & Drop D365 Invoice PDF

Drag your posted sales invoice PDF from D365 and drop it into the central dashboard zone. The client-side parser reads and extracts the bill-to, ship-to, item tables, and tax details instantly.

Step 02: GST Validation & Editing

Check the validation indicators. If the system flags a 0.01 rounding mismatch between D365 calculations and the IRP engine, or if an address exceeds the 100-character government limit, double-click the item grid cell to correct it on-screen. Special supply configurations (like SEZWOP or EXPWOP) will automatically bypass standard tax rules.

Step 03: Export JSON & Upload to IRP Portal

Click Download JSON to export a schema-compliant JSON file. Log into your government Invoice Registration Portal (IRP), upload the JSON file, and copy the approved response payload text returned by the government portal.

Step 04: Stamp IRN & QR Code back to PDF

Paste the government response payload into the stamping field in the right panel. e-Invoice Forge paints the official cryptographic QR code and 64-character IRN string directly onto your original invoice PDF layout, and saves the stamped copy to your local backup folder.
